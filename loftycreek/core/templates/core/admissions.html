Frequently Asked Questions

When should I apply for admission?

We recommend applying as early as possible, ideally by January for the following academic year. However, we accept applications throughout the year based on availability.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

Our average class size is 18 students per teacher, ensuring personalized attention and quality education for all students.

Do you offer transportation services?

Yes, we provide bus transportation services for an additional fee. Routes are available throughout the surrounding areas.
